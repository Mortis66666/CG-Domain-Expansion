  The Goal

The aim of this game is to expand your domain, the one with the larger domain wins.

  Rules

The game happens in a 7x7 board. Each player will control a sovereign.

Movements

During a player's turn, the player can get to move their sovereign 0 to 3 steps, with each step being a move to a direction either Up, Down, Left or Right. A sovereign cannot walk through a wall, to a cell with another sovereign, or outside the map.

Wall building

After each move, the player must build a wall around their sovereign, which can either be at Up, Down, Left or Right to the sovereign. After a wall is built, neither of both parties will be able to walk through it. A wall cannot be built on another wall, see "Expert Rules" section to see what will happen if you attempted to do it.

Win condition

A game is over if the walls on the map splits both players to two completely separated areas, meaning that one player can never reach to the other player. Note that the rule with player blocking a cell by standing on it does not count in this condition. The player with the most accessible cells wins.

  Expert Rules

If you attempt to reach a cell exceeding the 3-steps limit, you will move 0 steps instead.

If you attempt to build a wall on another existing wall, your wall build direction will be rotated clockwise until there's an empty space.

  Game Input

Initial input

Line 1: width height, the size of the board

Input

Line 1: x y, your position

Line 2: opponentX opponentY, opponent's position

Line 3: numWalls, number of walls

Next numWalls lines: ax ay bx by, position of two cells, a and b, denoting a wall between both cells, a is always either on top or on the left of b

Walls are given in chronological order
Output
A single line x y direction message, x and y denotes the cell you wish to go to, direction denotes the direction you wish to build your wall after moving to x y, message to print a message, optional.
direction is denoted by U, D, L or R
